A Day Exploring the Bikini Bottom ...

The Bikini Bottom is beneath Bikini Atoll located in the Pacific Ocean. The Bikini Bottom has a population of about 4.5 million citizens. The city has also been historically called Bikini Gulch, Dead Eye Gulch, and Bikini Bottomshire. The city is home to many different businesses, a stable economy, an education system, a structured government, etc.

When traveling to Bikini Bottom you might notice some of the city's well known citizens. I will explain who Spongebob and Sandy are soon. The others include: Patrick, Spongebob's bestfriend; Squidward, Spongebob's mean coworker and neighbor who plays the flute; Mr. Krabs, the CEO of the Krusty Krab who is in love with money; and Plankton, the owner of the Chum Bucket who is seen as a criminal. There is also Gary, Pearl Krabs, Mrs. Puff, and Karen Plankton.
